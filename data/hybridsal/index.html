HybridSal

HybridSal -- A language extension to SAL for specifying Hybrid Systems and a tool that performs Hybrid Abstraction (PDF) to automatically generate discrete SAL specifications that can be model checked by other SAL tools.

HybridSAL has been used in Symbolic Systems Biology to analyze hybrid systems with tens of continuous variables.
